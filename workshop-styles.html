THE CRAFTSMAN // pick a workshop

Four treatments of the craftsman's space, all fixing the scale problem: you and the craftsman now stand at matched size (armory-style zoom), and every design buries him in the wires, parts, and salvage of a man who never stopped taking machines apart. All code-drawn in the game palette. Every design keeps the working pieces: the PROBE LOG slate, the brass vice with the blue-pulsing implant, and room for the crank rig. Tell me a letter (or mix parts, like "B's wall with A's hanging bulbs") and I'll wire it into lesson1.js.
