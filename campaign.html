From the Wildwood to the Lab

Five arenas. You begin with a bow in the grass and fight through waves of the infected, unlocking a new kind of tech at each stage — weapons, then people, then structures, then the future — until you reach the lab where the outbreak began. Each card shows the battlefield, the hero you play, a marching enemy, and the gear you unlock there.

Concepts shown are the rough teaching hook per arena — we'll lock the full curriculum once the game's shape is set. Tell me what to adjust and I'll refine the art + wire the first arena into the game.
